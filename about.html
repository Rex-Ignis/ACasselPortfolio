About

I am Austin Cassel, an electrical engineer in training. I have a passion for tinkering, problem solving, repair, and electricity. I currently work at and attend Western Wyoming Community College in Rock Springs, Wyoming. I plan on producing a Vlog but have yet to have the desire to start it. I am constantly trying to fix different electronics and my car. I am a bibliophile and a huge fan of Frank Herbert's Dune series. I am a 3D modeler, using Solidworks and Fusion 360 for most of my designing. You can contact at through the links and Information at the footer.
